Based on the content of article 8 "ગાંધી સાર્ધ શતાબ્દી વંદના -8", a suitable short title would be:

"સત્યશોધક અને આંતર ચમત્કારના પ્રતિષ્ઠાપક"

This summarizes the main theme of the article, which focuses on Gandhi's self-description as a humble seeker of truth who doesn't claim to be a prophet or miracle worker. The article emphasizes his rejection of external miracles and instead highlights what Umashankar Joshi calls the "inner miracle" - Gandhi's ability to transform his life by following truth from the moment he understood it. The article portrays Gandhi as establishing a universal grammar for living that transcends religious boundaries and is accessible to all who make similar efforts.
