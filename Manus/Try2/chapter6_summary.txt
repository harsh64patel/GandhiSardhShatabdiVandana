Based on the content of article 6 "ગાંધી સાર્ધ શતાબ્દી વંદના -6", a suitable short title would be:

"સત્ય અને અહિંસાના અદના કાર્યકર"

This summarizes the main theme of the article, which focuses on Gandhi's humility in describing himself as merely an ordinary worker among many working for a great cause, rather than being compared to divine figures like Krishna. The article emphasizes his commitment to truth and non-violence as his only claimed virtues, and includes Joseph J. Doke's first impressions of meeting Gandhi, describing his surprising physical appearance, clear communication style, and transparent honesty.
