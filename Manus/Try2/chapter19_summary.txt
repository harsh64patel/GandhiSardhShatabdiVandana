Based on the content of article 19 "ગાંધી સાર્ધ શતાબ્દી વંદના -19", a suitable short title would be:

"ભજન આસ્વાદ અને ગાંધીજીના આંસુ"

This summarizes the main theme of the article, which continues Gandhi's interpretation of Narsinh Mehta's bhajan "Akhil Brahmandma Ek Tu Shri Hari" and shows his human side through an emotional moment. The article explores Gandhi's spiritual understanding of how God created the diverse world for divine joy and how humans can find God through love and nonviolence. It includes Manubhai Pancholi's observation that many educated Indians initially followed Gandhi reluctantly, not because they believed in his philosophy but because they saw his practical success. The article concludes with a touching anecdote about Gandhi shedding tears upon learning of his brother Karsandas's death, not because of the death itself but because his wish to see his brother one last time remained unfulfilled.
